Our Vision

"Uplifting Kalutara, the capital city of the District towards a pleasant town with good governance and sustainable development."

Our Mission

"Uplifting the life of the citizens of Kalutara City by providing municipal services that ensure safe reliable public welfare oriented and good health friendly environment for all the people living in Kalutara through upgrading the Municipal Council to the optimum place of the municipal administration by improving the performance of the institutional management and managing limited resources efficiently and effectively."
